MUSEUM OF CANDY

A unique museum showcasing different types of candies. We have everything from gaint candy bars, a doughnut wall (don't worry the doughnuts aren't real!),milkshake pool, and much more! Ever wanted to learn the history behind your favourite candy bar? Well wonder no more, we have educational session where you can learn how all these sweet delicacies came to be. Great place for the whole faimly to enjoy.

We also have weekly workshops where you can learn how to make different dishes, please see our calender for more information
